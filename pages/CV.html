Personal Profile

I'm a prehistoric hunter, living in nowadays South-Eastern Europe. I'm part of the Epigravettian culture, which was one of the last archaeological industries and cultures of the European Upper Paleolithic. It emerged after the Last Glacial Maximum around ~21,000 cal. BC or 19,050 BC, and is considered to be a cultural derivative of the Gravettian culture. Initially named Tardigravettian (Late Gravettian) in 1964 by Georges Laplace in reference to several lithic industries found in Italy,[4] it was later renamed in order to better emphasize its independent character. Our culture is the last stage of the Upper Paleolithic succeeded by Mesolithic cultures after 10,000 BP, so I can be considered the last European hunther gatherer.
